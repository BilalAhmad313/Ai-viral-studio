How To Make Viral Shorts

Hooks, pacing, retention, and practical templates to grow faster.

1) Hook (First 1–2 seconds)

Use curiosity, a bold claim, or show the final result immediately.

2) Pacing

Keep scenes short, use quick cuts, and remove unnecessary pauses.

3) Retention

Tell a mini-story: Setup → Problem → Solution → Payoff.

4) Template Idea

  • Hook: “I tested X so you don’t have to…”
  • Steps: show 3 quick steps
  • Result: show outcome
  • CTA: ask a question or prompt
