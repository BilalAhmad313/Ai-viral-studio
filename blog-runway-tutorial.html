Runway AI Tutorial (Step-by-step)

Image-to-video workflow for faceless AI video creation.

Step 1: Choose your Image

Pick a strong thumbnail image with clear subject and lighting.

Step 2: Write a motion prompt

Describe how the scene should move (camera, motion, mood).

Step 3: Generate variations

Try 3–5 variations and select the best clip.

Step 4: Export + Edit

Export the clip and edit in CapCut: subtitles, pacing, and music.
